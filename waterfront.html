Washington, DC Waterfronts

Just opened Fall of 2017, this massive $2.5 Billion development is the hottest new destination in the city, feturing an dazzling water views, state of the art residences, great restaurants, shops, marinas, water taxi, concert venue, and so much more. Located just about 2 miles from The GRAHAN, it is one of our shuttle bus destinations. Discover it for yourself.

Georgetown Waterfront - The Washington Harbor

Sitting on the oldest and historic neighbohood in DC, the Washington harbor offers an incredibly iconic scenery - view Key Bridge, Watergate Hotel, Kenedy Center, and the Arlington skyline accross into Virginia. Enjoy boat rides with live naration on the potomac. Features restaurants, luxury condos, offices, entertainment, and outdoor fun activities. Located about 3 miles from the GRAHAN.
